Welcome!  Like to sing?  Live in the greater Ann Arbor - Ypsilanti area? 

Whatever your skill level -- from beginner to expert -- here are many (free!) ways to join with people who simply want to sing together.

Tree-town Pub Sing  Literally, a traditional pub-sing.  50 - 100 folks gather, once a month, at an actual pub.  Anyone can lead a song; any song with a teachable chorus is welcome.  We lean towards 'traditional', folk, sea-songs, and the like -- but we get the occasional pop song or Disney song, too!  A great way to sing along with others, no matter how well you sing.
