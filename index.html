Tree-town Pub Sing  Literally, a traditional pub-sing.  50 - 100 folks gather, once a month, at an actual pub.  Anyone can lead a song; any song with a teachable chorus is welcome.  We lean towards 'traditional', folk, sea-songs, and the like -- but we get the occasional pop or Disney song, too!  A great way to sing along with others, no matter how well you sing.

Conor O'Neill's.
